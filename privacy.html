Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TradeKeepCRM ("we", "the app") helps solo service professionals manage clients, jobs, quotes, invoices, and follow-up reminders.

Data we collect

  • Account: email address (if you sign in with email) or Apple user identifier (Sign in with Apple)
  • Client data: names, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, notes, job/quote/invoice details, and payment amounts you enter
  • Files you upload: photos, documents, and voice notes attached to clients or jobs
  • Subscription status: managed via Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat

How we use data

  • Sync your business data across your devices via Supabase
  • Schedule local follow-up reminders on your device
  • Manage Pro subscription entitlements
  • Store attachments you choose to upload (photos, PDFs, voice notes)

Data storage & security

  • Client records are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security — only you can access your data
  • Auth tokens are stored securely on-device (Expo SecureStore)
  • Follow-up reminders are scheduled locally on your device
  • Files are stored in private cloud storage linked to your account

Data we do not collect

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use third-party advertising
  • We do not send SMS or email to your clients on your behalf

Third parties

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and In-App Purchases
  • RevenueCat — subscription receipt validation

Your rights

You may delete clients and records individually within the app, export your data from More → Export data, or contact us to request deletion of your account and all associated data.
