# Privacy Policy — TradeKeep

**Last updated:** June 2026

TradeKeep ("we", "the app") helps solo service professionals manage client information and follow-up reminders.

## Data we collect

- **Account:** email address (if you sign in with email) or Apple user identifier (Sign in with Apple)
- **Client data:** names, notes, appointment dates, payment amounts, and follow-up dates you enter
- **Subscription status:** managed via Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat

## How we use data

- Sync your client list across your devices via Supabase
- Schedule local follow-up reminders on your device
- Manage Pro subscription entitlements

## Data storage

- Client records are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security — only you can access your data
- Auth tokens are stored securely on-device via iOS Secure Enclave (Expo SecureStore)
- Follow-up reminders are scheduled locally on your device

## Data we do not collect

- We do not sell your data
- We do not use third-party advertising
- We do not send SMS to your clients (v1)
- We do not sync with calendars (v1)

## Third parties

- **Supabase** — authentication and database hosting
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple and In-App Purchases
- **RevenueCat** — subscription receipt validation

## Your rights

You may delete your account and all client data by signing out and contacting support to request account deletion, or by deleting clients individually within the app.
